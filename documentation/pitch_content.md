## Slide 2: The Problem
- **Header**: The Sharia Compliance Bottleneck
- **Points**:
    - **Manual Complexity**: Auditing 100+ page contracts against hundreds of AAOIFI rules is slow.
    - **Risk of Overlook**: Manual audits are prone to human error, leading to non-compliance risk.
    - **High Cost**: Specialist Sharia auditors have high operational costs and limited availability.
- **Key Insight**: Compliance is currently a speed bump, not a system.

## Slide 3: The Opportunity
- **Header**: Why AI? Why Now?
- **Points**:
    - **Speed**: Reduce audit time from hours to seconds.
    - **Accuracy**: 100% clause coverage with semantic rule matching.
    - **Scaling**: Allow JS Bank to scale Islamic portfolio without linear headcount growth.
- **Tagline**: Turning Compliance from a Constraint into a Competitive Advantage.

## Slide 4: Our Solution
- **Header**: An Intelligent Audit Guardrail
- **Visual**: 4-Step Pipeline Diagram
    1. **Ingest**: Multi-format PDF processing.
    2. **Decompose**: Breaking contracts into semantic clauses.
    3. **Align**: Matching clauses against AAOIFI FAS 28 via Vector DB.
    4. **Verify**: Explainable verdicts powered by Reasoning LLM.

## Slide 5: The AI Engine
- **Header**: Powered by Google Gemini & Pinecone
- **Details**:
    - **Reasoning**: Gemini-latest  Pro for nuanced Sharia interpretation.
    - **Retrieval**: Pinecone Vector DB for instant lookup across AAOIFI standards.
    - **Grounding**: RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) ensures zero-hallucination verdicts with direct citations.
    - **UI**: Premium Glassmorphic Real-time Dashboard (Next.js + Framer Motion).
